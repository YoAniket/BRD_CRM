# CRM_Business Analyst


The CG's CRM system is designed to streamline and automate the organization's customer relationship management activities, enabling efficient collaboration between Sales, Marketing, Customer Support, and Management teams.

The CRM manages the complete customer lifecycle, beginning with lead generation and continuing through lead qualification, opportunity management, sales closure, customer onboarding, post-sales support, and customer retention.

The high-level business process consists of the following activities:

* Prospective customers submit enquiries through various lead sources such as the company website, landing pages, social media campaigns, email campaigns, referrals, or manual lead entry.
* The CRM captures and validates the lead information while preventing duplicate records.
* New leads are automatically or manually assigned to the appropriate Sales Executive based on predefined assignment rules.
* Sales Executives contact the prospective customer, record communication history, schedule follow-up activities, and update lead status based on customer interactions.
* Qualified leads are converted into Customer Contacts and Sales Opportunities.
* Sales Executives manage opportunities through configurable sales pipeline stages by recording expected revenue, probability of closure, quotations, proposals, and customer interactions.
* Sales Managers monitor pipeline progress, approve quotations and discounts where required, review sales forecasts, and track team performance.
* Marketing teams execute campaigns, segment customers, nurture leads, and analyze campaign effectiveness through CRM reports.
* Customer Support Executives manage service requests and customer issues by creating support tickets, tracking SLA compliance, escalating unresolved cases, and recording resolutions.
* Business users generate operational and analytical reports to monitor lead conversion, sales performance, customer engagement, support metrics, and overall business performance.
* CRM Administrators manage users, roles, permissions, workflows, integrations, dashboards, reports, and overall system configuration to ensure secure and efficient platform operation.
Executive Management reviews real-time dashboards and business intelligence reports to support strategic decision-making and continuous business improvement
